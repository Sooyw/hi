Top 3 Hot Songs on The Billboard Hot 100

for the week of 29 February 2020

1.   The Box by Roddy Ricch

Roddy Ricch debuted at No. 1 on The Billboard Hot 100 with the song "The Box" from his highly anticipated debut album Please Excuse Me for Being Antisocial.


2.   Life Is Good by Future Featuring Drake

"Life Is Good" is the latest joint track of Future and Drake, ranked No. 2 on The Billboard Hot 100, showing that "Life Is Good" when Future and Drake collaborate.


3.   Circles by Post Malone

"Circles", the third single from Post Malone's third studio album Hollywood's Bleeding ranked No. 3 on The Billboard Hot 100.
